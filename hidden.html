It'll be fine

by Nico Llado

Every day felt different,

Only few words could describe

How time flew,

Without your love


Without your embrace

That kept me alive

Such moments I felt,

Love could heal me


The beat was strong,

Nothing has worn off,

Not your fresh smell,

Not your sunny look


Time was not right,

It ticked the other way,

It made me feel

That love could destroy me.
