MY LIFE'S JOURNEY(IMPLIED)

I woke up with a quiet morning. This is what I always experience in this wonderful paradise which I call "my home". Nowhere else to compare, nowhere else to come back for. It has simple features but a lot of memories are kept here.If one wants to know a kind, he/she has to find its home to know its true qualities and characteristics.

The beautiful trees that surround this paradise give me the vibe that I want to experience for the rest of my life.Could this be the option that I can resort to? This is the zone that keeps me safe and sound.

The leaves were noiselessly dancing. The movements were unpredictable as no path had been identified with certainty for every strike that it received from the wind. From that point I asked myself, where could I be headed to? Where could this wind take me? These questions were associated with my life's journey, which is quite blurry as of the moment.

The things that I do today could either lead me to something that I want or it won't for better reasons.
